About Me

I'm Nwala Lady-Joy, a passionate Web Developer and Data Analyst currently serving as a tutor at Pearls Africa Foundation. At the age of 17, I embarked on my journey as a tutor, guiding and inspiring young minds in technology. By 18, I proudly earned certification as a Data Analyst, demonstrating my dedication to mastering analytical skills early in my career.

Recently, at 19, I completed intensive training at Unilag Design Studio, expanding my expertise in electronics and digital fabrication. Driven by a relentless pursuit of knowledge, I'm eager to pursue higher education in computing department, aiming for future master's and PhD degrees. My technical skills encompass JavaScript, Materialize CSS, CSS, HTML, Power BI, and Excel, complemented by a strong foundation in social entrepreneurship and effective public speaking.

I thrive on solving complex challenges, from developing innovative technologies to addressing community needs through data-driven insights. Let's connect to explore how we can innovate and make a positive impact together.
